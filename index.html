I am a PhD student at State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Hujun Bao and Prof. Yuchi Huo. Previously, I received my BS and MS degrees from the Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT).

My research interest lies in realistic rendering/inverse rendering, 3D reconstruction, and computer vision.
