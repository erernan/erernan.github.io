Hello, I am Qi Wang, a PhD student at State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Hujun Bao and Prof. Yuchi Huo. Previously, I received my BS and MS degrees from the Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT).
